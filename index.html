pcodevsim

This browser app simulates the execution of p-code in a visual way meant to illustrate the inner workings and state changes of one possible p-machine for demonstrative or educational purposes. The simulation was developed by Jonathan Stone under the advisement of Dr. Thomas R. Turner. The p-machine was originally designed by Niklaus Wirth and modified by Dr. Thomas R. Turner as specified at http://cs2.uco.edu/~trt/cs4173/pspec.pdf (PDF).
